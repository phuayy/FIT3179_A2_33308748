Player Combat Profile

ACS (Average Combat Score): The best single measure of a player's combat impact, factoring in damage, kills, and more.

KPR (Kills Per Round): A pure metric for a player's fragging power, normalized across all rounds played.

Tactical & Consistency Profile

APR (Assists Per Round): Shows a player's contribution to team play, often through good utility usage.

KAST (%): A key consistency stat. The percentage of rounds a player had a Kill, Assist, Survived, or was Traded.

HS% (Headshot %): The most direct measure of a player's mechanical skill and aiming precision.
